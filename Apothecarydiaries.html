Name The Apothecary Diaries
Language Japnese
Released on Date 22 October 2023
Story Description

The Apothecary Diaries follows Maomao, a clever young apothecary who is thrust into the imperial court after being kidnapped. Using her medical knowledge and sharp wit, she solves palace mysteries while navigating court intrigue and earning the attention of a mysterious eunuch named Jinshi.
