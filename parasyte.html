Name Parasyte
Language Japnese
Released on Date 09 October 2014
Story Description

Parasyte: The Maxim tells the story of Shinichi Izumi, a teenager whose hand is taken over by a sentient alien parasite. As more parasites infiltrate society, Shinichi must fight to survive and protect humanity while grappling with his own transformation.
