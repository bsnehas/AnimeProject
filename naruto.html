Name Naruto: Shippuden
Language Japnese
Released on Date 03 October 2002
Story Description

Naruto follows Naruto Uzumaki, a spirited ninja with dreams of becoming the strongest leader of his village. As he trains and grows, he faces powerful enemies, uncovers secrets about his past, and learns the true meaning of friendship and perseverance.
