Name Dr Stone
Language Japnese
Released on Date 5 July 2019
Story Description

In the world of Dr. Stone, humanity is mysteriously petrified for thousands of years. When a brilliant teenager named Senku Ishigami awakens, he sets out to rebuild civilization using the power of science. With his friends by his side, Senku tackles ancient challenges with modern knowledge, sparking a thrilling journey of innovation, survival, and discovery.
